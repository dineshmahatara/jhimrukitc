Privacy Policy of Jhimruk Innovative Technology Center (JITC)


At Jhimruk Innovative Technology Center ("JITC"), we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By engaging with JITC's services or providing your information to us, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect
JITC collects only the following personal information:

Name: To address you appropriately in our communications.

Email Address: To send you relevant updates, responses, or information regarding our services.

Phone Number: To contact you directly when necessary for service-related inquiries or notifications.

We do not collect any additional personal information, such as financial details, browsing history, or sensitive data.

2. Purpose of Collecting Information
The information collected by JITC is used exclusively for the following purposes:

To respond to your inquiries.

To provide updates about our workshops, events, or services.

To notify you about any changes, new offerings, or other relevant information from JITC.

Your personal information will never be used for any other purposes, such as marketing, selling to third parties, or unrelated activities.

3. Data Protection Measures
JITC is committed to protecting the confidentiality and integrity of your personal information. We employ the following measures to ensure your data is secure:

Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to your data.

Data Encryption: Your data is stored securely using modern encryption methods.

Regular Reviews: We periodically review our security practices to identify and mitigate potential risks.

Despite our efforts, no method of data transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing of Information
JITC does not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Your information is used solely by JITC for the purposes outlined in this policy.

5. Your Rights
You have the following rights concerning your personal information:

Access and Correction: You may request access to your personal information or request corrections to ensure accuracy.

Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information from our records.

Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw your consent for us to contact you at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us via the methods provided below.

6. Retention of Information
JITC retains your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Once the information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
JITC reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated to you through email or posted on our official platforms. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
